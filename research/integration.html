Real-time integration of linguistic and non-linguistic information

In the recent decades, many researchers investigated the role of real-world knowledgdge in sentence processing (Altmann & Kamide, 1999; 2007; Chambers et al., 2002; Kamide et al., 2003), and the research trend was part of efforts to present alternatives to theories in which syntactic information was assumed to be perioritized by parsers (Chomsky, 1965, 1981; Frazier, 1979, 1987).
